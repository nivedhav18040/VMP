Threat context
Use multiple signals together—severity, exploit probability, exploit availability, and real-world activity—to sort what’s urgent, not just what’s “critical.”
Risk Score (0–10) A single composite priority score to drive sorting, filtering, and SLA-based workflows.
EPSS (0–1) Probability of exploitation in the next 30 days—fast-track remediation when risk crosses your threshold.
CVSS v3 + v4 context Use standardized severity with newer dimensions side-by-side as the industry transitions to v4.

Risk Visibility & Intelligence

Know what’s broken, and why it matters.

  • Vulnerability Assessment Continuously discover vulnerabilities across endpoints and network devices.
  • Vulnerability Prioritization Use exploitability and risk context to focus teams on issues attackers are most likely to target first.
  • High-risk software audit Get rid of unauthorized and unsupported software. Identify remote desktop sharing, end-of-life, and peer-to-peer software deemed unsafe—and uninstall them from endpoints in no time.

Audit & Compliance

Build security into every endpoint.

  • Security configuration management Use best-practice baselines to harden endpoints, prevent drift, and keep configurations consistently secure over time.
  • CIS compliance Assess posture against benchmark controls, identify violations fast, and act on guided remediation.
  • Policy customization Tailor checks to internal standards—adjust controls when “one-size-fits-all” doesn’t fit your environment.

Threat Mitigation & Response

Fix fast. Contain instantly.

  • Patch Management Deploy OS and third-party updates through controlled workflows—at scale.
  • Network Quarantine Isolate risky endpoints to contain active threats while remediation is in progress.
  • Zero-day Mitigation Apply mitigation actions quickly to reduce exposure while permanent fixes are evaluated.
